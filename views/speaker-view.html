Faith in Jesus Christ

Good morning, brothers and sisters. I'm grateful for the opportunity to speak with you today about faith in Jesus Christ.

In Hebrews 11:1, we learn that "faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen."

This definition helps us understand that faith is both a principle of action and of power. When we exercise faith, we are willing to act on our beliefs without having a perfect knowledge.

The Savior taught in Matthew 17:20 that even faith as small as a mustard seed can move mountains. This isn't necessarily about physical mountains, but about the mountains of challenges and trials we face in our lives.

Personal Experience

I'd like to share a personal experience that strengthened my faith...

  • Difficult health challenge last year
  • Prayers and priesthood blessings
  • Family support and service
  • Unexpected spiritual insights

Three Ways to Increase Faith

  1. Regular scripture study
  2. Heartfelt prayer
  3. Service to others

As we nurture our faith through these simple actions, we will feel closer to the Savior and more capable of following His example.

I testify that Jesus Christ lives and loves each one of us. He is aware of our struggles and stands ready to help us as we place our faith in Him.

In the name of Jesus Christ, amen.
